Do you Know Shah Rukh Khan Had A Different Name In Childhood ?

by: Ankit Singh

June 26, 2021

Bollywood • Entertainment

Follow on Telegram @ScoopBeatsNews

Shahrukh Khan had told about his childhood name in an interview. Shahrukh Khan’s childhood name is Abdur Rahman. This name was given to him by his maternal grandmother. However, this name was never registered. After this his father changed his name to Shahrukh Khan.

Shahrukh Khan says, ‘I don’t know any person named Abdur Rahman but my maternal grandmother named me Abdur Rahman in my childhood. My name not registered anywhere but she wanted my name to Abdur Rahman. I felt a bit strange right now. You think Abdur  in Baazigar, nothing suits Shahrukh Khan in Baazigar, he looks better.
